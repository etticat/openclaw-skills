## Substitution Rules

If an item is unavailable:
1. Search for same brand, different flavour
2. Search for same type, different brand
3. Skip and note in confirmation report

## Budget Priority (when over budget, cut in this order)

1. Remove treats/snacks first
2. Reduce quantities on bulk items
3. Never cut fresh produce
