## Starter Recommendations (by pillar)

**Communication / Confidence:**
- The Courage to Be Disliked — Kishimi & Koga (Adlerian psychology, separation of tasks)
- Never Split the Difference — Chris Voss (tactical empathy, negotiation)
- Crucial Conversations — Patterson et al. (high-stakes communication)
- Nonviolent Communication — Marshall Rosenberg (needs-based communication)

**Relationships:**
- How to Win Friends and Influence People — Dale Carnegie (genuine interest, listening)
- The Seven Principles for Making Relationships Work — John Gottman
- Attached — Amir Levine (attachment theory)

**Wellbeing:
- The Body Keeps the Score — Bessel van der Kolk (stress, emotions, and the body)
- Thinking, Fast and Slow — Daniel Kahneman (cognitive biases)

**Leadership / Productivity:**
- The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People — Stephen Covey
- The Hard Thing About Hard Things — Ben Horowitz (founder dynamics)
- Designing Your Life — Burnett & Evans (life design)

**Business / Strategy:**
- Zero to One — Peter Thiel
- The Lean Startup — Eric Ries
- Crossing the Chasm — Geoffrey Moore
